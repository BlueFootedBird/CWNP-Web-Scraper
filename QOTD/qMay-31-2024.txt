What operating system command may be used to determine the various nodes along the path between to network endpoints?
a:PING|b:PATHPING|c:NETSH|d:NSLOOKUP|x:I don't know
b
The correct answer is PATHPING. PATHPING and TRACEROUTE are two commands that can be used to identify the nodes along the route. PING, NSLOOKUP and NETSH do not provide this functionality.
