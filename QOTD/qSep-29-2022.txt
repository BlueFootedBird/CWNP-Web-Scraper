In an IEEE 802.11 RSN-enabled network, which key is used to derive all other keys used in the authentication process?
a:PMK|b:PTK|c:GTK|d:GMK|x:I don't know
a
A Pairwise Master Key (PMK) is the highest order key used within the 802.11 standard. The PMK may be derived from a Master Session Key exported by an Extensible Authentication Protocol (EAP) session or may be obtained directly from a Preshared key (PSK).
