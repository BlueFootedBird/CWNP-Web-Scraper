What are two common software Wireless LAN discovery utilities used for locating SSIDs, signal strength, channel use, and security?

NetStumbler and Kismet are tools used to locate and interrogate wireless LANs. L0phtCrack (LC5) and WinSniffer are used as password auditing and recovery tools. ShareEnum is a tool for discovering network shares and the permissions applied to those shares.
